Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: It's good for version control, and the branching/merging facilitates collaboration.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging creates a new conflict that combines branches will preserving the history, so there is room for conflicts, while rebasing reapplies the new commits onto another branch.
